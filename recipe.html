Khichdi recipe

Best Reciepe of Khichdi

Ingredients

  1. 1/3 cup Moong Dal
  2. 2/3 cup Rice
  3. 2 cup water
  4. 1 tbsp ghee
  5. 1 tsp cumin
  6. 1 tsp red chilli powder
  7. 1 tbsp salt

Method

  • Rinse Dal and Rice together well.
  • Put well washed mixture in cooker and add 2 cups of water
  • Add ghee, cumin, salt and chilliflakes to it.
  • Put the lid and put it on sim flame for 10 minutes.
  • Khichdi is ready and best served hot with pickle, curd and papad
